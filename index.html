RULES:

• Global Emission Rate (APY) was 100% at launch (except YFKA/ETH, which is always half).

• Global Emission Rate decays each Ethereum block.

• Decay rate is 1% every 5000 Ethereum blocks.

• When you first STAKE you lock in your personal emission rate for the selected pool. If you stake in all 4 pools, you will have 4 different personal emission rates.

• Each stake & every addition to a stake MUST BE A MINIMUM OF 0.4 YFKA WORTH OF LP TOKEN VALUE. (i.e. 0.2 YFKA + 0.2 YFKA worth of the other paired token). If you try to add less you will get an error and your wallet will show you an extremely high gas fee to send your transaction. DO NOT CONFIRM THE TRANSACTION, IT WILL FAIL!

• Once you lock in your personal emission rate, you retain that rate until you unstake or redeem. Meanwhile the Global Emission Rate continues to decay each block.

• SO LOCK IN AND STAY IN! Stack YFKA with higher emissions than everyone that locks in after you!

• There is a bonus pool, with 2X rewards. It switches between pools based on the lowest % of YFKA tokens staked. YFKA/ETH is never eligible for the bonus.

• You MUST claim bonus pool rewards while the bonus is active or else your accrued rewards will revert back to your standard ones.
